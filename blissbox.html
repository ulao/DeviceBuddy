Bliss-Box Update
To update the Bliss-Box you must press and hold down the reset button on the device. Once the button is pressed click the Pair button, and connect the 'unknown' device in the menu. After the pair is complete, use the update button to load and flash the device. Remember to keep the button held down until flashing starts.

Currently only GPA and 4-Play Advacned are supported in this tool.
